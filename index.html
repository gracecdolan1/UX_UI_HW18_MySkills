    • A bit about myself.

    I am Grace Dolan, a UX/UI designer. I specialize in User Research and developing creative and practical solutions. I have a project management background and a strong passion for design and technology. I am experienced in user research and prototyping. Some of the tools I use are Figma, AdobeXD, Miro, and Trello.

    Skills

    USER RESEARCH

    I conduct various types of research from user interviews to creating surveys. This allows me to gather sufficient user data to understand the problem at hand and brainstorm potential solutions.

    USER EMPATHY

    Empathizing with my users is crucial to being able to develop a user-focused solution. I use empathy maps primarily to understand my users thoughts and feelings about a problem.

    QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

    Interviewing my users is essential to my user research. Following a user interview plan, recording my interviews (with consent), and analyzing responses using an affinity diagram is a small part of my process.
